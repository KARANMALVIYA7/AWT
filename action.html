Take Action

Taking action against COVID-19 involves both individual and collective efforts

Vaccination

Vaccination stands as the linchpin of our defense against COVID-19. It not only fortifies an individual's immunity, shielding them from severe illness, and potential long-term effects but also plays a role in curbing the virus's overall transmission.

Follow Public Health Guidelines

These guidelines, which may vary by region, include wearing masks in public spaces, maintaining physical distance from others, and avoiding large gatherings to prevent the virus's transmission.

Stay Informed

It is important to regularly check updates from reliable sources such as the World Health Organization (WHO) and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and it is recommended to follow the directions of state and local authorities.
