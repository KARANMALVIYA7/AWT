About Corona Virus

The coronavirus, also known as COVID-19, is a highly contagious respiratory virus that emerged in late 2019, causing a global pandemic.

About More

Coronavirus what it is?

Coronavirus is a highly contagious virus responsible for the COVID-19 pandemic, characterized by respiratory symptoms and varying severity, with potential severe health implications. It spreads primarily through respiratory droplets.

About More

How to Protect Yourself

Protect yourself against COVID-19 by getting vaccinated, wearing masks, practicing good hand hygiene, and maintaining physical distance in crowded or indoor settings.

Wear Mask

Wearing a mask is an important measure to reduce the spread of COVID-19, as it helps prevent respiratory droplets containing the virus from entering the air and being inhaled by others.
Read More

Wash Your Hands

Washing your hands thoroughly and frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds is essential in preventing the spread of COVID-19, as it removes potential virus particles from your hands.
Read More

Stay at Home

Staying home and limiting non-essential outings, particularly when experiencing symptoms or during periods of high infection rates, is a key strategy to minimize the risk of COVID-19 transmission.
